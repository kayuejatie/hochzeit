The Dutch organist Gert van Hoef married Marjolein Speijer on November 17 in Voorthuizen. To mark the occasion, we were asked by Gert's friend Jeff DeCoe to make an embossed organ pipe as a wedding gift. It was crafted from 80 per cent tin by Terry Doyle and the inscription was by Terry Shires. We offer our congratulations to the couple

Gert van Hoef was born on June 17, 1994, in Barneveld, the Netherlands. At age 13, he received his first lessons from his grandfather. He received a digital organ from his parents and explored the instrument by himself. In June 2008, Gert started to study organ with Ms.Daamen, organist and choir accompanist in Barneveld.
